# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 9, 2026**

## Overview

Capsule ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal memory journaling app. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

- **Account information**: Email address and password (used for authentication)
- **Memories**: Text entries, mood selections, and dates you provide
- **Usage data**: Basic analytics (app opens, feature usage)

## How We Use Your Data

- **Storage**: Your memories are stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore) and associated with your account
- **AI Summaries**: When you use the Recaps or Chat feature, your memories are sent to Anthropic (Claude AI) to generate summaries and answer questions. This data is not stored by Anthropic beyond processing your request
- **Authentication**: Your email is used solely for account access

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Your data is shared only with:
- **Google Firebase**: For storage and authentication
- **Anthropic**: For AI-powered summaries (processed, not retained)

## Data Retention

Your memories are stored as long as your account is active. You can delete individual memories at any time from the Timeline tab.

## Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at mrcocotoasty@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

## Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Authentication is handled by Firebase Auth. Database access is restricted to authenticated users accessing only their own data.

## Children's Privacy

Capsule is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app.

## Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, email: mrcocotoasty@gmail.com
